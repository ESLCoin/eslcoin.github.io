Technical Launch

The goal of the technical launch is to raise awareness of the ESL Coin TRX token within the technical community, create a development fund for the Tron division team and build up a liquidity fund for the public launch of the token on major exchanges. The technical launch will be conducted through an initial auction series with a daily emission stating at 250k token a day and reducing every day. Unlike Initial Coin Offerings, the intended audience are technical investors/participants and developers who do believe in creating an ecosystem to disrupt the 80 Billion dollars language learning space. During the technical launch and anyone wanting to participate must interact directly with the smart contract and upon public launch a seperate dedicated UI will be created for interaction with the contract. To reward token particpants during this stage, 95% of the daily auction is shared among token stakers.

Technical participants can elect to be part of the charter members program and receive a share of day 1 and 2 auction revenue see charter members program.

Non technical partipants can partake in this project through other means. See our get invovled page for more information.

During phase 3 (CODENAME Graduation), ESL Coin TRX will be released to the public through a combination of an in house exchange and public crypto exchanges. A portion of the developer pool will be allocated to providing liquidity on exchanges.

Smart Contract Tutorial

Entering the Auction

Start by opening the contract interaction page and making sure your wallet is connected then follow the below instructions (Join the telegram for more assistance) The auction resets every day at 00:00 UTC. You may collect as soon as the auction ends.

Finding current day and total TRX pool (from all users) in the auction for the day, please note due to the calculations used, the total auction amount will show 1 million times the actual amount.

Collecting from the Auction

Collecting from the auction is a matter of going to the WRITE CONTRACT tab on the contract, go to command 10.xfLobbyExit and typing in the day you want to collect from.

Staking ESL Coin TRX

A stake can be started on the WRITE CONTRACT tab.

Go to command 5.stakeStart

Enter in amount of stakes desired followed by 8 zeroes (00000000). For example:

  • 10000 ESL = 1000000000000
  • 9001 ESL = 900100000000
  • 1337 ESL = 133700000000
  • 314 ESL = 31400000000

Colleting Ended Stakes

Grabbing your ended stakes is slightly more complicated. Just make sure your stake has completed fully.

  1. Start by opening the contract interaction page and making sure your wallet is connected
  2. Go to READ Contract and go to command 6.stakeCount and enter your wallet address
  3. Write down output. This is the number of active stakes you have +1
  4. Go to 3.stakeLists (Still at READ CONTRACT Tab) and type in stake number 0, this is stake #1 active for you
  5. copy output to a note pad
  6. repeat step 4 & 5 with your other stake numbers up to the # of total stakes you have -1 provided in step 3

  8. Go to WRITE CONTRACT and put in the stake information without commas (refer to imagae below)
